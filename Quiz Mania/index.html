About Quiz Mania

Welcome to Quiz Mania! 🎉

Quiz Mania is an interactive quiz platform that challenges your knowledge across various topics. Choose your difficulty level and dive into exciting quizzes!

⏳ Quiz Rules & Scoring

  • Each question has a **15-second** time limit.
  • If you don’t answer within **15 seconds**, the question is skipped.
  • You earn **1 point** for each correct answer.
  • Your total score is updated after each correct response.
  • Try to get the highest score and test your knowledge!

🎯 Difficulty Levels

  • Easy: Basic questions for beginners.
  • Medium: A mix of tricky and fun questions.
  • Hard: For quiz masters looking for a challenge!

📚 Quiz Categories
